Website Redesign case study

Harmony House Redesign

HH for Cats, a non-profit rescuing and rehoming cats, needs a website redesign for better user experience and increased engagement with potential adopters and donors.

Read the case study

Challenge

We observed that the Harmony House for Cats website isn’t meeting the needs of users because of the lack of readability, organization, and navigation structure which causes people to feel unsure about the organization and left without the resources they need to continue with the adoption process.

Original Website

Solution

To redesign the website so it’s more engaging and visually appealing to ensure a trustworthy relationship with future adopters that helps inform them about how they can welcome a cat into their furr-ever family.
